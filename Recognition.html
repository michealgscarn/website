Sponsors

We would like to thank the Bosch community fund for providing us with financial support. Their generous donation has allowed us to purchase the new VEX sensors and cover our building materials. The new VEX color sensors have allowed us to create an auto sorter for more efficient programs and more ease in driving. The funding for tournaments has allowed us to travel to different states to still compete, despite the limitations put on by COVID-19. Without the support from Bosch, we would not have had the same amazing season we have had so far.

Mission

"We are motivated by the desire to develop products that are “Invented for life,” that spark enthusiasm, that improve quality of life, and that help conserve natural resources."

Impact on Our Team

The Bosch community fund has allowed us to purchase many much needed materials for our robot. We have used their generous donation to get the new sensors released by VEX. These sensors have greatly increased the accuracy and efficiency of our robot and autonomous. We are using the new color sensors to automatically sort balls during the autonomous period of VEX "Change Up." We are also using the new distance sensor, which allows us to get accurate location measurements during both competition and skills autonomous periods. Because of Bosch's donation, we are able to learn more about programming and designing for our robot.

Without P&G’s support our season and robot would have been much different than it has been so far.
