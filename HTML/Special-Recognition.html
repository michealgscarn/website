Special Recognition

We would like to thank the Bosch community fund for providing us with financial support. Their generous donation has allowed us to purchase the new VEX sensors and cover tournament fees. The new VEX color sensors have allowed us to create an auto sorter for more efficient programs and more ease in driving. The funding for tournaments has allowed us to travel to different states to still compete, despite the limitations put on by COVID-19. Without the support from Bosch, we would not have had the same amazing season we have had so far.

We would also like to thank P&G Core fund for providing us with financial support. Their charitable donation has allowed us to purchase the newly legal flex wheels. These wheels have helped us to create a new and better intake. This has greatly improved the speed and efficiency of intaking and descoring balls.Without P&G’s support our season and robot would have been much different than it has been so far.
