History

In 1886, an apprentice named Robert Bosch opened a workshop for Precision Mechanics and Electrical Engineering in Stuttgart. In the mid- 1980s, things rapidly took off and unstoppably. From 1897, Bosh became the only supplier of a truly reliable ignition in cars by developing a better designed magneto ignition. Additionally, in 1913, bosch produced headlights, generators and regulators for vehicles to improve the safety of the driver. Things took a turn for the worse in 1942 when Bosh facilities where bombed repeately. Despite this huge setback, Bosch rebuilt and designed household devices and automobile applications. Bosh has continued to build quality and safe products to this day to boost our quility of life
