Our Team

We are a group of students who created a private robotics team based in Union, Kentucky. We dedicate ourselves to excellence in everything that we do. We set ambitious goals every year to learn to build and program something new. in Elizabethtown inside of the engineering labs. We currently have three teams that compete in the VEX Robotics competition being 4104A, 4104B, and 4104C. We also share our VEX Robotics program with Central Hardin and John Hardin High School who is mainly coached by Jason Neagle. Using our knowledge of the design process, our teams will attempt to design and build a robot that is capable of winning the VEX Robotics competition.

​

For the sake of the future of our Vex Robotics program, we plan on trying to excel in Vex Robotics in every way possible by becoming experts in the engineering process, know what it is like to be an engineer, and try to learn from our failures and our victories. We also try to introduce the sport of Vex Robotics to others to try to help create potential engineers and help them find something they never knew they wanted.
