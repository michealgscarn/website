About Us

Team

We are two students who created a robotics team in Union, Kentucky. We dedicate ourselves to excellence in everything that we do. We set ambitious goals every year to learn to build and program something new. Although we are not associated with any school program, we compete on the national and global level every year. We volunteer for many local programs to share our passion for STEM with future engineers. We often collaborate with our local teams to increase interest in STEM in our community. Every year we use our knowledge to design and build a new robot for the new VEX Robotics Compeition game.

​

In the future, we plan to continue to learn new aspects of STEM and keep sharing our knowledge with our community. We want to learn more about the design process and how to apply it to our robotics team. We also want to learn more about engineering as it relates to the VEX Robotics Competition program. We will learn from our victories and take much more away from our failures. More than anything, we want to continue volunteering and encouraging future engineers to participate in STEM.

Mission

Through Challenges and Successes, we will strive to innovate and improve our understanding of the design process

Vision

Our vision is to create more oppertunities for the youth in our communities to gain exposer to STEM. Our communiity involvement will always keep this vision in mind.

Values

Hard Work
We embrace the idea that excellence can mean going above and beyond. We don't quit until our objective has been met.

Coopertive Competition
We are fiercly competitive but an unfair win is not a win at all.

Innovation Sharing
We reconize that sharing ideas improves the entire robotics community.

Data-driven
Testing and results drive our decisions, not other teams.

Positive Attitude
Through victories and defeats, we will always focus on human kindness
