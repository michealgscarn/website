41091A Intro

We are a team two aspiring engineers who attend Cooper High School in Northern Kentucky. We engage ourselves in Vex Robotics every day in order to improve our live skills of teamwork, problem solving, and perservierance as well as enhancing pur knowledge in the various fields of engineering and computer science. Our VEX Robotics program takes place in our house where we build, program, document, and drive our robot. We as well try to support the teams we compete against in competitions as much as we can.
